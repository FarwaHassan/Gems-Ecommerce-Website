Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a Crystal and a Gemstone?

Crystal is the raw natural formation of a mineral as found in nature, whereas, Gemstone is the cut and polished form of the mineral.

Are pearls the same as minerals?

Pearls are obtained from living organisms, therefore, not the same as rock minerals in their composition. Gemologists categorise them as Semi-Precious Organic Gems.

How do you tell if the gemstone is real?

The easiest way is the touch test. A natural stone is always cold to touch. Then look inside the gemstone through a 20x loop to see if there are any visible natural inclusions. Take measurements like Refractive Index, Birefringence and Specific Gravity of the gemstone using special equipment for each. Lastly, if still not clear, check the gemstone under a microscope to see any signs of naturally occuring formation lines and/or inclusions.
